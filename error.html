Errors are a very disturbing part of any operation. As much as we have limited system errors to the barest minimum, we have included this section to ensure that you know the right steps to take if you come across an error.

SYSTEM ERRORS

These may include:

  • Webpage not loading properly.
  • Webpage not loading at all.
  • Webpage displaying an error or confusing message.
  • Clicking a button but not getting any response.
  • Webpage is hanging.
Possible Solutions:
  • Refresh the webpage.
  • Clear your browser’s local cache under the browsers settings.
  • Check your internet connection to ensure it is stable.
  • Use another browser
  • Contact Support.

EMAIL/ PHONE MESSAGE ERRORS

These may include:

  • Receiving an empty mail/ phone message.
  • Receiving a mail/ phone message with an error or confusing message.
  • Not receiving any mails/ phone messages.
Possible Solutions:
  • Check your registered email address and phone number to ensure it is correct.
  • Check/ ask your mobile phone network provider if the DND (do-not-disturb) function is activated on your mobile phone.
  • Check your internet connection to ensure it is stable enough for you to receive mails.
  • Check your mobile phone network provider to ensure you have been receiving other messages.
  • Contact Support

ACCOUNT ERRORS

These may include:

  • You cannot log into your account.
  • Your password is refusing to update.
  • You changed your password but still cannot log in.
  • Your correct username/ password combination is returning an error.
Possible Solutions:
  • Ensure that you have verified your account.
  • Cross check your registered username and password sent to you in your welcome email.
  • Ensure your Browser has not stored a wrong username / password for the webpage. You may have recently changed your password and not updated the browser’s saved password.
  • Reset your password
  • Contact Support.

For other errors or system failures not recorded here, please contact support by submitting a new support ticket on your login page or sending an email with your account details and complaint at support@kompletecare.com
