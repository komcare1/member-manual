TO REGISTER A NEW MEMBER ACCOUNT

  • Visit www.kompletecare.com/
    member_registration
  • Enter all necessary information.
  • Read and accept the terms of use and privacy policy.
  • Click the “REGISTER” button.
  • Check the mailbox of your registered email, open up the KompleteCare welcome mail and click the verification link provided. If the link is not clickable, copy and paste this into your browser.
  • Your account will then be verified and you can now log in.

Important

“Ensure that you register with an email address and phone number that you have access to as regular updates will be sent to you via these”

TO LOG INTO YOUR ACCOUNT

  • Visit www.kompletecare.com/member_login
  • Enter your username and password
  • Click log in
*Please note that if your account has not been verified or you enter a wrong username/ password combination you will be denied access.

TO RESET YOUR PASSWORD

  • Visit www.kompletecare.com/member_login
  • Click the forgot password link below the "login" button
  • Enter your email address and verify your account from the mail sent to your mailbox
  • Enter a new password
  • Click reset

GETTING STARTED

Once your account has been successfully verified, you can now log in.
On logging in, you will be redirected to your dashboard where you can view or update your profile, create hospital cards, view appointments and access doctor information. On the navbar you will find links to view new or older messages, book new appointments and visit the dashboard.

The very first thing to do is to update your profile.
Setting your password recovery question and answer as well as your mobile phone number is a good place to start.
