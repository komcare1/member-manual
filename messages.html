Doctors and Hospital administrators can send you necessary information through the messages page. You can also send messages across to Patients, Doctors and other Hospital Administrators.
You can view your pending, past and sent messages history on the MESSAGES page. Simply click the "Messages" link on the navigation bar of your Member Dashboard.

Kindly note that this is different from the Live chat feature that enables you send messages live to Doctors.

TO VIEW AN OLDER OR NEW MESSAGE

To view a new or older message, follow these simple steps:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • Click the "Messages" link on the navigation bar lof your Member Dashboard
  • Click the subject of the message you wish to read

TO COMPOSE A MESSAGE

To compose a new message, follow these simple steps:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • Click the "Messages" link on the navigation bar lof your Member Dashboard
  • Click the "Compose Doctor Message" or "Compose Admin Message" link at the top right and left corners of the messages page
