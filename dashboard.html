PROFILE

Your basic profile is created once you register on the KompleteCare platform. To view your profile, simply click on the "PROFILE" link on your dashboard.

You can also update information on your profile. You can add spoken languages for specific language preference in appointments, update your email, phone number, date of birth, occupation, gender, as well as set your password recovery question and answer.
To update your profile:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "PROFILE" link
  • Click the "UPDATE PROFILE" button under the table
  • Update the desired fields and click the "UPDATE" button

ONLINE CONSULTATION

Online consultaions are categoried into video and voice consultations.
These allow you to engage in video/ audio consultation with Kompletecare Doctors from the comfort of your home or office. You may get advice on what to do in an emergency situation, double check any health information, receive disease prevention and health maintenance consultation, pre-international travel consultation, receive second opinion consultation from expert or a standalone consultation on minor medical consultation. The online consultation also helps you receive pre-hospital consultation for conditions that will definitely require you to go to a hospital.
It is mandatory to limit all video/ audio calls to a period of 30 minutes. If you require further help afterwards or if the phone call gets disconnected for any reason, the Doctor can reach such through the chat platform.

To make an audio call:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "ONLINE CONSULTATION" link
  • Click the "AUDIO" button (You can use a referral code to get a 5% discount if you have one)
  • Enter payment details into the payment gateway and pay
  • At the Online Consultation Centre, sort through the list of available Doctors and select your desired Doctor
  • Click the audio call icon to enter the call room
To make a video call:
  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "ONLINE CONSULTATION" link
  • Click the "VIDEO" button (You can use a referral code to get a 5% discount if you have one)
  • Enter payment details into the payment gateway and pay
  • At the Online Consultation Centre, sort through the list of available Doctors and select your desired Doctor
  • Click the video call icon to enter the call room

HOME-BASED CONSULTATION

A Home-based consultation is a premium Kompletecare™ service that allows Kompletecare Members (patients) book private medical sessions in their home with a Doctor.
This means that a member would rather have a private medical session with a Doctor in the comfort of their home/office, than in the hospital.
Because of the nature of this service, it is mandatory that a Doctor have initial dialogue with any patient desiring or requesting home based consultation. This dialogue may occur through our voice/ video consultation, hospital appointment or any personal contact with such patient. For a Member to book a consultation, the Doctor is required to send a home consultation invite from your dashboard. While the charge for home-based consultation is at the discretion of the Doctor, please note that the minimum charge for such consultations is NGN10,000.
Once the Doctor issues an invite, the patient is notified immediately and prompted be asked to pay the charge.
To accept a home-based consultation invite, simply follow the steps below:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOME-BASED CONSULTATION" link
  • Under pending invites, click the "ACCEPT" button necxt to the invite you wish to accept
  • Enter the necessary details and click the "PAY" button and pay online (You can use a referral code to get a 5% discount if you have one)

HOSPITAL APPONTMENT

A Hospital appointment is a Kompletecare™ service that allows Kompletecare Members (patients) book premium hospital appointments with a Doctor in the Doctor's registered place of practice. This service is peculiar only to Doctors attached to a medical institution. Patients are mandated to open hospital cards for your hospital before they can book hospital appointments with a Doctor. They may create multiple hospital cards with a single Kompletecare Member account allowing them to book appointments in any hospital of their choice.
To book a hospital appointment:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • Click the "Book Appointment" link on the Navigation bar
  • Select a Sort type
  • Select your Doctor, date, time and appointment type (You can use a referral code to get a 5% discount if you have one)
  • Click the pay button and pay online

HOSPITAL CARDS

Hospital cards are virtual cards created to enable you book hospital appointments with a Doctor in his/her place of practice. Members are mandated to open hospital cards for their desired hospital before they can book hospital appointments with a Doctor in that hospital. They may create multiple hospital cards with a single Kompletecare Member account allowing them to book appointments in any hospital of their choice.
To create (open) a hospital card

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOSPITAL CARDS" link
  • Click the "CREATE CARD" button
  • Select your desired hospital and enter all necessary details and click the "SUBMIT" button
Once a hospital card has been created, you can proceed to book a hospital appointment without further delay.

MEDICAL RECORDS

You may need to upload certain records concerning your patient’s health or certain results from any medical tests your patient was involved in. These are all stored as medical records. Medical records may include:

  • Test results
  • Progress notes
  • Orders and prescriptions
  • Assesment and plan
  • Physical examination
  • History of present illness
  • Growth chart and developmental history
  • Immunization history
    • And any other related records...
Please ensure that these records are sent to you through this medium only with your approved consent. They are not to be shared with any third party.
To access a medical record sent to you
  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "MEDICAL RECORDS" link
  • Click on the link under "file" next to the file you wish to download

HOW DO I COMPLETE AN APPOINTMENT OR CONSULTATION?

Kompletecare hospital appointments and home-based consultations must be marked as complete immediately after the appointment/consultation. This is proof that the appointment/consultation has taken place.
To mark an appointment as complete:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOSPITAL APPOINTMENT" link
  • Under "Pending Appointments", click the icon under "Complete" next to the appointment you wish to complete
  • Click "YES" under "Do you wish to proceed?"
  • Enter ratings for the appointment and click the "SUBMIT" button
To mark a home-based consultation as complete:
  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOME-BASED CONSULTATION" link
  • Under "Pending Consultations", click the icon under "Complete" next to the consultation you wish to complete
  • Click "YES" under "Do you wish to proceed?"
  • Enter ratings for the consultation and click the "SUBMIT" button

I CANNOT FIND THE APPOINTMENT/CONSUTATION I WISH TO COMPLETE

For an appointment/ consultation to be complete, both Doctor and Member (patient) involved must mark as complete. If the Doctor marks the appointment/ consultation as complete before you do, it will be removed from the "pending" appointments/consultations list.
However an email and text message notification will be sent to you to enable you complete the appointment/consultation. Simply click the link in the mail/text message. If you recieved neither email nor text message after the patient completed the appointment, contact support.

HOW DO I CANCEL AN APPOINTMENT/CONSULTATION

You can cancel any appointment/consultation you are no longer interested in. However hospital appointments/home-based consultations are paid for immediately after booking and getting a refund depends on how far you have gone with the process.
Please note that you are assured an 80% refund of your money ONLY if you cancel your consultation at least 24 hours before the fixed consultation time. You cannot undo this action.
To cancel a hospital appointment

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOSPITAL APPOINTMENT" link
  • Under "Pending Appointments", click the icon under "Cancel" next to the appointment you wish to complete
  • Click "YES" under "Do you wish to proceed?"
  • Select the reason you are cancelling the appointment and click the "SUBMIT" button
To cancel a home-based consultation
  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "HOME-BASED CONSULTATION" link
  • Under "Pending Consultations", click the icon under "Cancel" next to the consultation you wish to complete
  • Click "YES" under "Do you wish to proceed?"
  • Select the reason you are cancelling the consultation and click the "SUBMIT" button

HOW DO I COMPLETE AN ONLINE CONSULTATION

Just like our hospital appointments and home-based consultation, it is mandatory for both Doctor and Member (patient) involved in an online (audio or video) consultation t mark such consutation as complete. For online consultations, you simply need to add ratings for the consultation to mark it as complete. To do this, follow the steps below:

  • After your audio or video consultation, click the "END SESSION" button at the top right corner of our call room
  • Enter your ratings on the ratings page and click the "SUBMIT" button

WHAT HAPPENS IF NO DOCTOR ATTENDS TO MY ONLINE CONSULTATION

We have Doctors ready on the go to attend to your video or audio consultations. However, if for some reason no Doctor was able to attend to you, you can simply stop the session to continue later at no extra cost.
To do this, click the "BACK TO DASHBOARD" button at the top left corner to go back to your dashboard and log out to resume the session at a later time. To resume a previous session:

  • Login to your Member Account
  • On your dashboard, click the "ONLINE CONSULTATION" link
  • Under the "YOU HAVE INCOMPLETE CALLS" header, click the "CALL BACK" to complete your incomplete call
  • You will be redirected to the Online consultation center without having to pay again
